Our Story

Long-Time Locals Who Genuinely Care

As long-term residents of Southwest Florida, we saw a real need to support the people in our community. We built A Helping Hand of Florida to provide personalized, trustworthy help — not a call center, not a franchise. Just dedicated neighbors who care about your wellbeing.

Our mission is simple: to make your life easier, one day at a time.
